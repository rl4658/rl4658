### 👨‍💻 About Me

I am a passionate software engineering student at the **University of Western Ontario (Class of 2025)**. I focus on building intelligent systems, interactive applications, and bridging the gap between complex backend logic and user-centric design.

* 🎓 **Education:** B.E.Sc. in Software Engineering (GPA: 3.7/4.0, Dean’s Honour List)
* 🚀 **Currently Exploring:** Advanced HCI methodologies, 3D graphics algorithms, and containerized AI deployments on Google Cloud Platform.
* 🔬 **Research & Projects:** Developing AI-powered transcription pipelines (Vosk, RoBERTa), researching prompt-engineering for software testing workflows, and designing AI-driven music generation experiences.
* 🏆 **Honors:** 1st Place at Western Cyber Society Hackathon (AI playlist personalization) & 2nd Place at Western Engineering Competition.
